Welcome to New Harvest Fellowship Church

"So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand" Isaiah 41:10

We are a non-denominational apostolic movement church, desiring to keep it simple for everyone to encounter the saving grace of Jesus Christ.

Isaiah 61:1-2a

We warmly welcome you to come get the word of God and see how life changes as you have Jesus in you...

Youth carry a message...

The Youth Ministry at New Harvest Fellowship Church serves youth aged 14-30, guiding them into a life-giving relationship with Jesus. We have weekly meetings every Monday from 4:30 PM to 6:00 PM at our church, as well as special events like our monthly 'Revive Night.' We encourage youth to use their unique gifts to serve the church and community.

Children are the gifts from God...

Our Children's Ministry focuses on teaching children about the love of Jesus in a fun and engaging way. We provide safe and nurturing environments for children aged 4-14, addressing their spiritual, emotional, and physical needs every Saturday from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Breakfast and lunch are provided
