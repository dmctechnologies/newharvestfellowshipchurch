Our Mission

Becoming a biblical-centered church that inspires people to passionately follow Jesus!

We achieve this by:


1. Reaching Up in dynamic and passionate worship to God

2. Reaching Out to the unchurched people with the message of hope found in Christ alone

3. Reaching In through intentional discipleship and the study of God's Word

Our Vission

Our vision is to become a simple gathering of people who learn and practice to worship and love God, love one another, love our neighbors, and reach out to the lost world with the saving grace of Jesus Christ through the gospel and sharing resources to uplift the impoverished.

Our Cooperative Goals

  • To worship the triune God
  • To have fellowship with believers
  • To reach the unreached people groups (e.g., Muslims, traditionalists, army barracks, prisons, police stations, street children, prostitutes, villages)
  • To minister to the needy, orphans, widows, etc.
  • To train leaders and plant churches

Reaching in

We are committed to discipling our congregation to change everyday conversations into gospel conversations. We equip our members to share their faith and win souls for Christ, including sharing the gospel with Muslim friends and neighbors. We also focus on training leaders, planting churches, and evangelizing children, youth, and couples.

Reaching out

Our outreach involves taking the Gospel to our neighbors through crusades, door-to-door outreaches, and evangelism efforts in prisons, army barracks, clan men, witch doctors, bars, marketplaces, and schools. We also address the physical needs of orphans, child-mothers, widows, the homeless, and those suffering from poverty.
